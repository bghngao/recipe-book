<div class="lang" data-lang="en">
  <h1 class="title-recipe">🍰 Roll Cake Sponge</h1>

  <!-- Yield -->
  <div class="container">
    <p><strong>Yield:</strong> Makes 1 roll cake (27cm square baking tray)</p>
  </div>

  <!-- Ingredients -->
  <div class="container">
    <h2 class="title-ingredient">Ingredients</h2>
    <div class="ingredient-quantity">
      <div class="column-left-ingredient">Eggs</div>
      <div class="column-right-amount">3, room temperature</div>
    </div>
    <div class="ingredient-quantity">
      <div class="column-left-ingredient">Granulated sugar</div>
      <div class="column-right-amount">60g</div>
    </div>
    <div class="ingredient-quantity">
      <div class="column-left-ingredient">Cake flour</div>
      <div class="column-right-amount">60g</div>
    </div>
    <div class="ingredient-quantity">
      <div class="column-left-ingredient">Unsalted butter</div>
      <div class="column-right-amount">20g</div>
    </div>
    <div class="ingredient-quantity">
      <div class="column-left-ingredient">Milk</div>
      <div class="column-right-amount">1 tbsp</div>
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- Instructions -->
  <div class="container">
    <h2 class="title-instruction">Instructions</h2>
    <ol>
      <li>Bring eggs to room temperature. Sift the cake flour. Line a 27cm square baking tray with parchment paper and preheat the oven to 180°C.</li>
      <li>Crack the eggs into a bowl and beat lightly. Add 1/3 of the sugar and whip with a hand mixer until pale and fluffy. Add the remaining sugar in two stages, whipping after each addition, until the batter falls in thick ribbons that hold their shape on the surface.</li>
      <li>Sift the flour again directly into the bowl. Gently fold with a spatula using a lift-and-cut motion from the bottom — work quickly but carefully to preserve the air in the batter.</li>
      <li>In a small bowl, melt the butter and milk together over a hot water bath. Pour along the edge of the batter and fold in quickly using the same lift-and-cut motion.</li>
      <li>Pour the batter into the lined tray and smooth the surface with a spatula. Lift the tray and drop it from about 5cm to release large air bubbles. Repeat 3 times. Bake for 10–15 minutes, or until the surface is lightly golden and springs back when gently pressed.</li>
      <li>Remove from oven and drop the tray once from about 10cm to prevent shrinking. Carefully lift the sponge (with parchment) onto a cooling rack. Once cooled to room temperature, peel off the parchment and wrap the sponge in plastic wrap to keep it moist until ready to fill and roll.</li>
    </ol>
  </div>
</div>

  <!-- 作り方 -->
  <div class="container">
    <h2 class="title-instruction">作り方</h2>
    <ol>
      <li>卵を常温に戻す。薄力粉はふるっておく。天板にクッキングシートを敷く。オーブンを180°Cに予熱する。</li>
      <li>ボウルに卵を割り入れて軽くほぐす。砂糖を1/3量加え、ハンドミキサーでふんわりするまで泡立てる。残りの砂糖を2回に分けて加え、そのつど泡立てる。生地をすくって落としたとき、リボン状に表面に積もるくらいになるまで泡立てる。</li>
      <li>薄力粉を再度ふるいながらボウルに加える。底から持ち上げて切るように、泡をつぶさないよう手早くさっくりと混ぜる。</li>
      <li>別のボウルにバターと牛乳を入れ、湯煎で溶かす。生地のふちに回し入れ、同じく底から持ち上げて切るように手早く混ぜる。</li>
      <li>クッキングシートを敷いた天板に生地を流し入れ、スパテラで表面を平らにならす。天板を5cmほどの高さから3回落として大きな気泡を抜く。180°Cのオーブンで10〜15分焼く。表面が薄くきつね色になり、軽く押してすぐ戻れば焼き上がり。</li>
      <li>オーブンから取り出し、天板を10cmほどの高さから1回落として縮みを防ぐ。クッキングシートごとケーキクーラーの上に移す。粗熱が取れたらクッキングシートをはがし、ラップで包んで乾燥を防ぐ。クリームを塗って巻くまでそのままおく。</li>
    </ol>
  </div>
</div>
