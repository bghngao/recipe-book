<div class="lang" data-lang="en">
  <h1 class="title-recipe">🍡 Butter Mochi</h1>

  <!-- Yield -->
  <div class="container">
    <p><strong>Yield:</strong> Makes 1 pan (9x13-inch / 22x33cm), about 24 squares</p>
  </div>

  <!-- Ingredients -->
  <div class="container">
    <h2 class="title-ingredient">Ingredients</h2>
    <div class="ingredient-quantity">
      <div class="column-left-ingredient">Mochiko flour</div>
      <div class="column-right-amount">450g</div>
    </div>
    <div class="ingredient-quantity">
      <div class="column-left-ingredient">Granulated sugar</div>
      <div class="column-right-amount">300g</div>
    </div>
    <div class="ingredient-quantity">
      <div class="column-left-ingredient">Baking powder</div>
      <div class="column-right-amount">8g</div>
    </div>
    <div class="ingredient-quantity">
      <div class="column-left-ingredient">Salt</div>
      <div class="column-right-amount">3g</div>
    </div>
    <div class="ingredient-quantity">
      <div class="column-left-ingredient">Eggs</div>
      <div class="column-right-amount">4, room temperature</div>
    </div>
    <div class="ingredient-quantity">
      <div class="column-left-ingredient">Whole milk</div>
      <div class="column-right-amount">450mL, room temperature</div>
    </div>
    <div class="ingredient-quantity">
      <div class="column-left-ingredient">Coconut milk</div>
      <div class="column-right-amount">400mL</div>
    </div>
    <div class="ingredient-quantity">
      <div class="column-left-ingredient">Unsalted butter</div>
      <div class="column-right-amount">115g, melted</div>
    </div>
    <div class="ingredient-quantity">
      <div class="column-left-ingredient">Vanilla extract</div>
      <div class="column-right-amount">10mL</div>
    </div>
    <div class="ingredient-quantity">
      <div class="column-left-ingredient">(Optional) Shredded coconut</div>
      <div class="column-right-amount">to taste</div>
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- Instructions -->
  <div class="container">
    <h2 class="title-instruction">Instructions</h2>
    <ol>
      <li>Preheat oven to 180°C. Butter a 9x13-inch (22x33cm) baking pan and line with parchment paper.</li>
      <li>In a large bowl, whisk together the sugar, baking powder, and salt. Sift in the mochiko flour and whisk to combine.</li>
      <li>Add the eggs, milk, coconut milk, melted butter, and vanilla extract. Whisk until the batter is completely smooth with no lumps.</li>
      <li>Pour the batter into the prepared pan. Sprinkle shredded coconut evenly over the top if using.</li>
      <li>Bake for 50–60 minutes, or until the top is deep golden brown and a skewer inserted in the centre comes out clean.</li>
      <li>Remove from oven and allow to cool in the pan for 15–20 minutes before cutting.</li>
      <li>Cut into squares and serve. Store leftovers covered at room temperature for up to 2 days.</li>
    </ol>
  </div>
</div>

  <!-- 作り方 -->
  <div class="container">
    <h2 class="title-instruction">作り方</h2>
    <ol>
      <li>オーブンを180°Cに予熱する。9×13インチ（22×33cm）の天板にバターを塗り、クッキングシートを敷く。</li>
      <li>大きめのボウルにグラニュー糖、ベーキングパウダー、塩を入れて泡立て器で混ぜる。もち粉をふるい入れ、さらに混ぜ合わせる。</li>
      <li>卵、牛乳、ココナッツミルク、溶かしバター、バニラエッセンスを加え、ダマがなくなるまでよく混ぜる。</li>
      <li>生地を型に均等に流し込む。ココナッツファインを使う場合は表面に均一に散らす。</li>
      <li>表面がこんがりときつね色になり、中心に竹串を刺してきれいに抜けるまで50〜60分焼く。</li>
      <li>オーブンから取り出し、切り分ける前に型のまま15〜20分冷ます。</li>
      <li>正方形に切り分けて盛り付ける。余った分は蓋をして常温で2日以内に食べきる。</li>
    </ol>
  </div>
