<div class="lang" data-lang="en">
  <h1 class="title-recipe">🍌 Banana Bread</h1>

  <!-- Yield -->
  <div class="container">
    <p><strong>Yield:</strong> Makes 1 loaf (9x5-inch / 23x13cm pan)</p>
  </div>

  <!-- Ingredients -->
  <div class="container">
    <h2 class="title-ingredient">Ingredients</h2>
    <div class="ingredient-quantity">
      <div class="column-left-ingredient">Ripe bananas</div>
      <div class="column-right-amount">3 large</div>
    </div>
    <div class="ingredient-quantity">
      <div class="column-left-ingredient">All-purpose flour</div>
      <div class="column-right-amount">190g</div>
    </div>
    <div class="ingredient-quantity">
      <div class="column-left-ingredient">Granulated sugar</div>
      <div class="column-right-amount">150g</div>
    </div>
    <div class="ingredient-quantity">
      <div class="column-left-ingredient">Unsalted butter</div>
      <div class="column-right-amount">75g, melted</div>
    </div>
    <div class="ingredient-quantity">
      <div class="column-left-ingredient">Egg</div>
      <div class="column-right-amount">1</div>
    </div>
    <div class="ingredient-quantity">
      <div class="column-left-ingredient">Vanilla extract</div>
      <div class="column-right-amount">1 tsp</div>
    </div>
    <div class="ingredient-quantity">
      <div class="column-left-ingredient">Baking soda</div>
      <div class="column-right-amount">1 tsp</div>
    </div>
    <div class="ingredient-quantity">
      <div class="column-left-ingredient">Salt</div>
      <div class="column-right-amount">1/4 tsp</div>
    </div>
    <div class="ingredient-quantity">
      <div class="column-left-ingredient">(Optional) Walnuts or chocolate chips</div>
      <div class="column-right-amount">80g</div>
    </div>
  </div>

  <!-- Instructions -->
  <div class="container">
    <h2 class="title-instruction">Instructions</h2>
    <ol>
      <li>Preheat oven to 175°C. Grease a 9x5-inch loaf pan and line with parchment paper.</li>
      <li>In a large bowl, mash the bananas until smooth. Stir in the melted butter.</li>
      <li>Mix in the sugar, egg, and vanilla extract.</li>
      <li>Sprinkle in the baking soda and salt. Stir to combine.</li>
      <li>Fold in the flour until just incorporated — do not overmix. Fold in walnuts or chocolate chips if using.</li>
      <li>Pour the batter into the prepared pan and smooth the top.</li>
      <li>Bake for 55–65 minutes, or until a skewer inserted into the centre comes out clean.</li>
      <li>Cool in the pan for 10 minutes, then turn out onto a rack to cool completely before slicing.</li>
    </ol>
  </div>
</div>

  <div class="container">
    <h2 class="title-instruction">作り方</h2>
    <ol>
      <li>オーブンを175℃に予熱する。パウンド型にバターを塗り、クッキングシートを敷く。</li>
      <li>大きめのボウルにバナナを入れ、なめらかになるまでつぶす。溶かしバターを加えて混ぜる。</li>
      <li>砂糖、卵、バニラエッセンスを加えて混ぜる。</li>
      <li>重曹と塩を加えてよく混ぜ合わせる。</li>
      <li>薄力粉を加え、粉気がなくなるまでさっくりと混ぜる（混ぜすぎない）。くるみやチョコチップを使う場合はここで折り込む。</li>
      <li>生地を型に流し込み、表面を平らにならす。</li>
      <li>175℃のオーブンで55〜65分焼く。中心に竹串を刺してきれいに抜けたら焼き上がり。</li>
      <li>型のまま10分冷ました後、ケーキクーラーに取り出して完全に冷めてからスライスする。</li>
    </ol>
